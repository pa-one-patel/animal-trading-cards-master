Red Panda

The red panda (Ailurus fulgens) is a furry mammal with a lush red coat, a bushy tail, and a masked face.The red panda is more closely related to raccoons than to giant pandas.

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Size: 20-25 inch body;
  • Lifespan: 8-10 years
  • Basic Animal Group: Mammal

A red panda is about as big as a domestic cat. Its body ranges from 20 to 25 inches and its tail is 11 to 23 inches. Males are slightly heavier than females, with the average adult panda weighing 6.6 to 13.7 pounds.
